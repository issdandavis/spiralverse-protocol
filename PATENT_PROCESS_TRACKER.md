## üîí Safe Mode Progression Framework

### Patent Testing & Validation Stages

#### Stage 0: Concept (Safe Mode)
- **Environment**: Isolated sandbox, no external connections
- **Testing**: Paper validation, theoretical proofs
- **AI Involvement**: Single AI agent drafts initial concept
- **Output**: Preliminary specification document
- **Risk Level**: MINIMAL

#### Stage 1: Initial Draft (Safe Mode)
- **Environment**: Controlled dev environment
- **Testing**: Unit tests, mock implementations
- **AI Involvement**: 2-3 AI agents collaborate on draft
- **Output**: Complete technical specification
- **Risk Level**: LOW

#### Stage 2: Implementation Validation (Semi-Safe Mode)
- **Environment**: Staging environment with limited real data
- **Testing**: Integration tests, security audits
- **AI Involvement**: Full AI swarm (5-8 agents)
- **Output**: Working prototype + test results
- **Risk Level**: MODERATE

#### Stage 3: Prior Art & Legal Review (Semi-Safe Mode)
- **Environment**: Public databases, legal review systems
- **Testing**: Novelty validation, claim differentiation
- **AI Involvement**: Legal-AI leads, supported by Research-AI
- **Output**: Prior art report + patentability opinion
- **Risk Level**: MODERATE

#### Stage 4: Provisional Filing (Launch-Ready)
- **Environment**: USPTO filing system
- **Testing**: Final document review, filing checklist
- **AI Involvement**: Legal-AI + human attorney collaboration
- **Output**: Filed provisional patent application
- **Risk Level**: CONTROLLED HIGH

#### Stage 5: Full Utility Filing (Launch)
- **Environment**: USPTO + PCT international systems
- **Testing**: Complete claims review, international requirements check
- **AI Involvement**: Multi-jurisdiction Legal-AI coordination
- **Output**: Full utility patent application(s) + international filings
- **Risk Level**: MANAGED PRODUCTION
